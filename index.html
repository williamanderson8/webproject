The Art of Tattoos

Tattoos are more than just ink on skin, they’re powerful forms of self-expression, storytelling, and cultural tradition. Whether minimalist or fully detailed, tattoos often hold deep personal meanings for those who wear them.

Why People Get Tattoos

People get tattoos for many reasons, to honor loved ones, mark important life events, express creativity, or simply because they enjoy the aesthetic. Tattoo culture has grown globally and has roots in ancient practices from many civilizations.

Types of Tattoos

  • Realism
  • Watercolor
  • Geometric
  • Minimalist
